# 6. Damage, Death, and Return

This section defines how damage is dealt, how a hero dies, and how that hero returns to play.

## 6.1 Damage Sources

damage is dealt by effects, including innate attacks and skills.

## 6.2 Damage Types

damage is one of 3 damage types: physical damage, magic damage, or spirit damage.

## 6.3 Physical Damage

Whenever a hero takes physical damage, check knockback.
Resolve knockback by following §5.4.

## 6.4 Magic Damage

magic damage has no additional rule at this time.

## 6.5 Spirit Damage

spirit damage has no additional rule at this time.

## 6.6 Taking Damage

When a hero takes damage, add that damage to that hero's taken damage.
taken damage remains on that hero until a rule removes it.
A hero dies when its taken damage is equal to or greater than its max health.

## 6.7 Death Resolution

When a hero dies, finish resolving the current effect first.
Then resolve death in this order:
1. Move all of that hero's pitched cards and cards in hand to the bottom of your skill deck.
2. Remove all taken damage from that hero.
3. Move that hero to your hero pool zone.
4. That hero is no longer deployed.
5. That hero becomes dead.
6. That hero's death timer becomes equal to the number of times that hero has died this game.
7. That hero is not ready.
8. Attuned cards stay with that hero.

## 6.8 Death Timer and Return

A hero with an active death timer is dead, is not ready, and cannot deploy.
When that hero's death timer reaches 0, that hero stops being dead and becomes ready.
When that hero next takes a turn, you may deploy it by following §4.2.
