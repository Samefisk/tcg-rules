# 0. Overview

Read this section first. It tells you what you are trying to do and how the rest of the rulebook is organized.

## 0.1 Objective

You win the game if your heroes have all 4 arcane items equipped.
Your heroes may equip those arcane items collectively.
You must also have one of your heroes occupy the tile with the arcane field.
A player who satisfies both conditions wins immediately.

> **What This Means:** Every other rule exists to support this race. You gather all 4 arcane items and control the center at the same time.
